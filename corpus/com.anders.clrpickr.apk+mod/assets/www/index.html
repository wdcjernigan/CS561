clrpickr automatically generates color palettes by averaging the colors within an image, either from the camera, or by picking a pre-existing image from your gallery.

These color palettes are then returned as HEX, RGB and CMYK color values, which can be :

  • copied to your clipboard
  • Shared as plain text
  • Emailed as palette via Gmail

The colors generated should be used as a guide or for inspiration and may not exactly represent colors in image
